REVIEW

By: Gerson Vargas

October 26, 2025

Legend (2015) is a bold and gritty crime drama that showcases Tom Hardy's incredible versatility as he portrays both Reggie and Ronnie Kray-two real-life twin gangsters who dominated London's underworld during the 1960s. The film mixes style and substance, offering an inside look at the brothers' rise to power and their complicated relationship. IMDb

Director Brian Helgland balances slick cinematography with dark emotional core. Hardy's dual performance is magnetic, making each brother feel completely distinct. While Legend delivers plenty of sharp dialogue and brutal action, its strongest moments come from the psychological tension and loyalty between the Krays.

Trivia

  • Tom Hardy wore subtle makeup differences to distinguish Reggie and Ronnie Kray while filming both roles in the same scenes.
  • The bar scenes were filmed in real London locations once frequented by the actual Kray twins.
  • Emily Browning, who plays Frances, recorded narration inspired by real interviews with the Krays' acquaintances.
