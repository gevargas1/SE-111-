REVIEW

By: Gerson Vargas

October 26, 2025

The Long Walk is a dark, gripping exploration of human endurance and morality under pressure. The movie skillfully adapts Stephen King's novel (written under his pseudonym Richard Bachman) balancing suspense with emotional storytelling.

Its pacing and atmoshpere keeps viewers on edge, and the performances bring real emotion to a brutal story about choice, survival, and sacrifice.

Trivia

  • The novel was originally published under the pen name "Richard Bachman".
  • Lead actor Cooper Hoffman and his co-stars walked nearly 400 miles filming the walking scenes, in harsh heat and on concrete.
  • Stephen King called it one of his favorite works.
