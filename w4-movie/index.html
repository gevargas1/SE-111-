Home

Set in a dystopian America, The Long Walk follows fifty teenage boys chosen to compete in a deadly endurance contest. They must walk at least 3 miles per hour—if they slow down too long or too often, they are “eliminated." The last walker standing wins anything he desires. The film explores psychological endurance, fear, and the cost of survival.

Legend

Legend is a British crime drama film that tells the rise and fall of the notorious Kray twins-Reggie and Ronnie-who rules London's East End in the 1960s. Played by Tom Hardy in a dual performance, the film captures glamour, violence, and brotherly tension that shaped their empire.

Company Credits: StudioCanal, Working Title Films, Cross Pictures

Release Date: September 9, 2015

Genres: Biography, Crime, Drama

Rating: R

Running Time: 2h 12m
